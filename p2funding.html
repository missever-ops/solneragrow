P2 Funding

Investment Strategy and Allocation of Objectives

Solnera has secured P2 investment, co-financed by the European Union, to accelerate the development of Solnera One — a smart aeroponic device with AI-powered growth optimization and adaptive LED technology. This funding enables us to transform our innovative concept into a market-ready, sustainable, and user-friendly solution for urban indoor gardening and food self- sufficiency.


Our P2 investment will be strategically deployed across key areas:

1. Engineering and Product Development – Expanding technical capabilities, including staff costs, to refine Solnera One’s adaptive LED system, integrate AI-based growth optimization, and ensure seamless IoT connectivity.

2. Material Sourcing and Manufacturing – Securing sustainable, high-quality components and optimizing production processes for durability and scalability.

3. Market Preparation and Promotion – Supporting the creation of marketing content and establishing strategic partnerships to ensure a successful product launch.
