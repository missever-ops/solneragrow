Solnera One - Illuminate Your Growth Potential

Solnera One is a smart aeroponic device with innovative LED system that optimizes plant growth while reducing energy consumption.

Its core is adaptive LED technology, which, with advanced sensors and AI, automatically adjusts growing conditions to each plant’s stage of growth. This optimizes growth, improves quality, and reduces electricity use.

Designed for urban residents seeking fresh, home-grown food despite limited space, time, or knowledge, Solnera One makes indoor gardening easy and sustainable. Featuring a sleek, modern design, it integrates technology, sustainability, and style, while its app allows switching between growth and ambient lighting modes to enhance usability in living spaces.

Solnera demonstrates that technological innovation can align with environmental responsibility, offering a practical solution for urban self-sufficiency that respects space, nature, and users.
